Fennekin (Japanese: フォッコ Fokko) is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VI. It evolves into Braixen starting at level 16, which evolves into Delphox starting at level 36. Along with Chespin and Froakie, Fennekin is one of the three first partner Pokémon of Kalos available at the beginning of Pokémon X and Y.
