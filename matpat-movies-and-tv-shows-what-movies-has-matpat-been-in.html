MatPat Movies and TV Shows: What movies has MatPat been in?

An American YouTuber and Internet personality by the name of MatPat was born Matthew Robert Patrick on November 15, 1986. He founded the YouTube channels The Game Theorists, The Film Theorists, The Food Theorists, and The Style Theorists, each of which examines different video games, films, TV shows, and online series, as well as various foods and clothing.

Patrick not only makes his channels, but he also narrates the vast majority of the videos that are posted there. Along with hosting the YouTube Premium series MatPat’s Game Lab, Patrick also founded the gaming channel GTLive.

Patrick had gathered more than 40 million subscribers as of August 2023, and his five channels had received more than 8 billion cumulative views.
