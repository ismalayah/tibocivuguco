The subject of this article has no official English name.
The name currently in use is a fan romanization of the Japanese name.

(Japanese: サトシ Satoshi) is the main character of Pokémon Zensho, who is directly based on Red of the original games. Like his counterparts, he has a rival based on Blue: Shigeru.

History

Satoshi first appears in Prologue: Pallet Town, beginning his journey on the same day as Shigeru. He starts off with Charmander, Shigeru with Squirtle. They have their first battle, which Satoshi wins.

Satoshi obtains all eight Badges and challenges the Pokémon League, where he sweeps through the Elite Four quickly and defeats his rival, Shigeru, who already beat the Elite Four, for the final time.

Satoshi pushes Shigeru out of the way of a falling pillar, damaged in their last battle by Shigeru's Blastoise, and the two renew their friendship, pledging to capture Mewtwo together.

Pokémon

On hand

Jigglypuff
Satoshi captures Jigglypuff around the time he battles Blaine. Satoshi has Jigglypuff lull Articuno to sleep that Blaine might capture the legendary bird. The circumstances of its capture are never explored.

Jigglypuff's only known move is Sing.

DebutCinnabar Island
Alakazam
Satoshi's Alakazam first appears when Satoshi sends it out to battle Agatha's Gengar. The circumstances of its capture are never explored.

None of Alakazam's moves are known.

DebutIndigo Plateau

In the Box

Magikarp, Slowpoke, Krabby, and Psyduck
Satoshi receives many Magikarp (at least three), a Slowpoke, a Krabby, and a Psyduck from a fisherman in return for his Dratini while at the Safari Zone. They are all sent to his Box afterwards.

None of these Pokémon's moves are known.

DebutFuchsia City

Formerly owned

Dratini
Dratini is a Pokémon that Satoshi found in the Safari Zone by fishing. He gives it to a fisherman afterwards, in exchange for several Water-type Pokémon (see above).

None of Dratini's moves are known.

DebutFuchsia City
Nidoran♂
Satoshi catches a Nidoran♂ while the Pokémon is getting the mail. However, the Nidoran♂ ends up being a metamorphosed Bill. Bill returns to his human self when Satoshi places him in a machine that separates his human self from his Pokémon self.

None of Nidoran♂'s moves are known.

DebutVermilion City

Trivia

  • He shares his name with Ash Ketchum from the Pokémon anime. However, he is based on Red, not Ash, so his name actually comes from the optional names from the game. A similar situation applies to Shigeru, Satoshi's rival and Haruka from Pokémon Chamo-Chamo ☆ Pretty ♪.
  • Satoshi suffers a complete loss to Shigeru in their second rematch; despite the comic being based almost entirely on the games, this situation actually runs counter to them, because, in the games, victory is necessary to proceed.
  • Unlike Ash's Pikachu, Satoshi's Pikachu voluntarily uses a Thunderstone on itself in order to evolve into Raichu; this is in preparation for the battles against the Elite Four.
    • Satoshi is Red's only counterpart to own a Pikachu, and permanently evolve it into Raichu.
