The Lunala Treat (Japanese: ルナアーラのおやつ Lunala Snack) is a Key Item introduced in Generation IX. It is one of the treats obtained from Snacksworth, which are used to encounter Legendary Pokémon in Paldea.

Effect

The Lunala Treat allows the player to encounter and battle Lunala at Casseroya Lake. Showing the Lunala Treat to Snacksworth will have him provide the player a hint to Lunala's location.
