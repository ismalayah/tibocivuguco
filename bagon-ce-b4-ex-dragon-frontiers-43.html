Bagon δ (Japanese: タツベイ δ Tatsubay δ) is a Water-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Dragon Frontiers expansion.

Card text

Granite Head
いしあたま		10
During your opponent's next turn, any damage done to Bagon by attacks is reduced by 10 (after applying Weakness and Resistance).

Trivia

Origin

Granite Head's Japanese name is that of Rock Head, an Ability in the Pokémon games that Bagon can have.
