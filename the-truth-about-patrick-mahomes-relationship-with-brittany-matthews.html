Inside Patrick Mahomes And Brittany Matthews' Relationship

While fielding questions from her Instagram followers during an April 2022 Q&A, Brittany Matthews seemed taken aback by the number of fans who were asking her about a potential second pregnancy; after all, she'd only been married a month by then. "Ok, y'all really out here wanting us to have another baby," she wrote (via E!). "We don't exactly know yet! Y'all need to calm down." But despite her indecisiveness at the time, she found herself making a pregnancy announcement on Instagram just one month later.

"Round 2!" Matthews captioned a family photo of herself, Patrick Mahomes, and their daughter Sterling, who frowned while gripping a sign that read, "Big sister duties coming soon." Speaking to USA Today Sports about the happy news, Mahomes said, "Hopefully we get a boy, but I'll love him or her either way. It's going to be awesome."

Mahomes ended up getting his wish. Matthews shared some footage of their gender reveal party on Instagram, which began with shots of a number of their guests guessing that she had a son-bun in the oven. She and Mahomes learned the sex of their second child by squirting water guns filled with blue liquid at a sign reading, "Baby Mahomes: Boy or Girl???" Upon seeing blue splatter appear all over the words, an overjoyed Mahomes celebrated by leaping into a pool fully clothed.
