Sacred Fire (move) - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

published on July 03, 2024
From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Sacred Fire (Japanese: せいなるほのお Sacred Flame) is a damage-dealing Fire-type move introduced in Generation II. Prior to Generation VI, it was the signature move of Ho-Oh. Effect Sacred Fire inflicts damage, with a 50% chance of burning the target. If the user is frozen, using Sacred Fire will thaw out the user and deal damage to the target in the same turn.
