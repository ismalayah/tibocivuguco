Put a Ring On It: Joseline Hernandezs Fans Applaud Her for Keeping Balistic on His Toes

It’s clear that a lot of people want Joseline Hernandez and her boyfriend DJ Balistic Beats to get married.

Because many expressed that last month when the Puerto Rican Princess posted a photo of herself and Beats on Instagram. The shot showed the couple at a restaurant, which Hernandez captioned “Date night with the bae.”

In the comments section of that post, requests for the couple to tie the knot came pouring in, and much of that same chatter came when they were seen in a promo clip for “Marriage Boot Camp: Hip Hop,” which they co-star in this season.

“This is my baby Balistic Beats,” Hernandez said in the video. “We’ve been together for two years on and off, and we here ’cause he got to put a ring on that thing before I find the next best thing.”

“You what?” Beats asked her afterward, as he gave her a look.

The clip was posted on Wednesday, Feb. 12, and it’s already been viewed over 150,000 times.

In the comments section, many cheered on Hernandez and said they loved that she gave Beats an ultimatum. Others said they hope the music producer pops the question, because he and Hernandez make a wonderful couple.

“TELL HIS A– JOSELINEEEEE💀🤣,” one person wrote.

“I love y’all together @ballistic_beats_ put a ring on it,” another person pleaded.

“He lookin at you like ‘f–k you mean next best thing’ 😂😂😂😂,” another comment read.

There was also one fan who said they desperately want to see the “Love & Hip-Hop: Miami” star in a healthy relationship due to all she’s been through.

Because much of the tumultuous relationship between Hernandez and her ex Stevie J, whom she shares a daughter with, played out on “Love & Hip-Hop: Atlanta” some time ago.

“I heard that Joseline hahaha helll yea what man wouldn’t marry U?” that person wrote. “tf he tripping already gurl I hope u find everything u want and need im tired of u not having the right one I wanna see U happy all the way u r a beautiful boss ok @joseline 💯😍💙.”
